Yorkshire Terrier

Yorkshire Terriers, or Yorkies, are small but confident dogs known for their energetic and bold personalities. Despite their tiny size, they are fearless and often act as though they are much larger than they are. Yorkies are intelligent, quick learners, and make excellent companions for active individuals or Their silky, fine coats require regular grooming, and they tend to form strong bonds with their owners

Features of Yorkies:

  1. Small Size: Yorkies are compact, typically weighing between 4-7 pounds,
    making them ideal for apartment living.
  2. Energetic and Confident: Despite their size, they are bold and full of energy,
    often displaying a larger-than-life attitude.
  3. Intelligent and Trainable: Yorkies are quick learnersbr and can pick up commands and tricks easily with proper training.
  4. Long, Silky Coat: Their fine, silky fur requires regular grooming to keep it looking healthy and tangle-free.
